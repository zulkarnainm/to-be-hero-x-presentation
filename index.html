Core Concept

In the world of To Be Hero X, heroes aren't born—they're shaped by belief. The deeper the public's trust in a hero, the stronger that hero becomes. This unique "Trust Value" system creates a fascinating dynamic world where popularity directly translates into superhuman abilities.

Plot Summary
